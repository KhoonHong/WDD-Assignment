East Malaysia

Borneo, the world's third largest island, which consists of the Malaysian states of Sabah which is closer to the Philippines than to mainland Malaysia, Sarawak in the west, and the Federal Territory of Labuan. Labuan is an island in its small archipelago of the same name due north of Brunei; its closest land mass is with Sabah. It lies to the east of Peninsular Malaysia (also known as West Malaysia), the part of the country on the Malay Peninsula. The two are separated by the South China Sea.
