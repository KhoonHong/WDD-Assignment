MATTA Fair 2018
15 / 3 / 2018

Read More

Our Travel ARC are had take part in the MATTA Fair 2018 at Kuala Lumpur. MATTA Fair is an annual travel fair that is held nationwide to encourage the public to travel more, either around or outside the country. Organized by the ‘Malaysian Association of Tour and Travel Agents’ (MATTA), the fair, which takes place between March and September every year, offers some of the best deals for anything travel-related, such as tours, hotel stays and flight tickets.
