Penang

Welcome to Penang

Penang is a state of Malaysia that comprises of the island of Penang, and the adjacent land on the Malay peninsula. It is one of the most popular places to visit in Southeast Asia, with the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Georgetown being the major draw card.























Most of the state of Penang is located on an island and as such you can visit famous old British buildings,temples , old chinese-style buildings coupled with a big city experience. This part of Malaysia is also known for its food, and anyone who loves to eat will truly find a culinary paradise here, much of which is down to the mix of influences in the city.
